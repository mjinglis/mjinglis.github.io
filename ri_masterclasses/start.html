Welcome
As a participant in the Royal Institution's Masterclass Programme, you are requested to take part in a study designed to investigate how attending the masterclasses influences reasoning behaviour. Taking part in the study involves answering a short series of reasoning questions online at four points during the year.

All the information you provide will be treated as confidential and will only be used for scientific purposes. You will be identified only by initials and birth date, and no one at the Royal Institution will be able to see your responses; they will be stored and analysed independently by a research team at Loughborough University.

It is very important that you answer these questions on your own. Just try your best. The questions will take around 15 minutes to complete, so please ensure you have sufficient time before starting.
