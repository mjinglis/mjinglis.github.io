Thankyou. You have just taken the Wason THOG Task, named after British psychologist Peter Wason. It is designed to investigate people's logical reasoning techniques.

This study is attempting to discover whether training in symbolic logic has any effect upon people's responses to Wason's task. Two groups were asked to take part: mathematics students (experimental group) and childhood studies students (control group).
